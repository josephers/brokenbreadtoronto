Directions

Parking

Street Parking - ample street parking is available all along Yonge St, Park Home Ave, and Beecroft Rd. Parking is free until 1pm on Sundays
Hotel Parking - Hotel Novotel provides underground parking for a flat rate of $5.00 until 5pm. Parking validation is not offered

Children's Church (Kindergarten to Grade 6)

Room: Dempsey Room, 2nd Floor
Time: Children are dismissed from the main worship immediately following the Welcoming portion of our service (about 11:15am) to receive more personalized care and instruction.

Infants and Toddlers

Currently, infants and toddlers remain with their parents in the main worship. We have a dedicated area within the main worship room for parents and babies.
For more information contact: kids@brokenbreadtoronto.com
